Welcome to the Dunbar Project

Dunbar School was completed in January 1918 , for the purpose of educating the students . The School was named after Paul Laurence Dunbar a renowed american-african poet . American-African children in first through the ninth grades attended the Dunbar School until 1951 , when de jure segregation was eliminated from the school systems of the State of Arizona. When segregation in the AriZona was eliminated , Dunbar School became the none segREGATED jOHN sPRING jUNIOR high School , and conitnued as much until 1978 when the school was closed permanently .
