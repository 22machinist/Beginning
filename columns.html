Welcome to Dunbar Project

Dunbar School was completed in January 1918 , for the purpose of educating Tucson's African-American Students . The school was named after Paul Laurence Dunbar , a renowned African-American Poet African-American Students in the first through ninth grade attended the school until 1951 , when de jure segregation was eliminated from the school systems of th The State of Arizona When segregation in the state of Arizona is eliminated , Dunbar School became the non-segregated John Spring Junior High School , and continued as such until 1978 when the school was closed permanently .
